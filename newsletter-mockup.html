Destination of the Week

The Amalfi Coast in Shoulder Season

The crowds thin, the lemon groves perfume the air, and the Mediterranean is just warm enough for an afternoon swim. Late May and early October are the secret months on Italy's most photographed coastline, and a handful of properties still have rooms.
