Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Wayline Travel™ (“Wayline Travel,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website at waylinetravel.com, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. If you have any questions, you can reach us at info@waylinetravel.com or connect with the founder on LinkedIn.

Who we are

Wayline Travel™ is an independent travel advisory affiliated with FORA Travel. We design and book luxury travel experiences for our clients. Garrett Roseberry is the founder and primary advisor, and can be reached directly on LinkedIn.

What information we collect

We collect only the information you choose to share with us, in the following ways:

  • Newsletter signup. When you enter your email address into the “Guided Compass” subscription form on our site, that address is passed to our newsletter platform, Beehiiv, to complete your subscription. Beehiiv stores your email and sends you the newsletter on our behalf.
  • Trip inquiries via FORA. When you click “Start Your Journey” or any other intake button on our site, you are taken to a secure intake form hosted by FORA Travel at secure.foratravel.com. Any information you enter there (name, email, travel preferences, budget, etc.) is collected by FORA Travel under their privacy policy and shared with us so we can plan your trip. By submitting the FORA intake form, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy and FORA Travel’s privacy practices.
  • Direct email. If you email us at info@waylinetravel.com, we receive and store that correspondence in our email inbox so we can reply and follow up.
  • LinkedIn messages. If you reach out to Garrett on LinkedIn, your message and any information you share are subject to LinkedIn’s own privacy policy and are handled by us only to respond and continue the conversation.
  • Website analytics. We may use a privacy-friendly analytics tool (such as Vercel Analytics or Plausible) to understand which pages are most useful to visitors. These tools collect aggregated, anonymous information such as page views, device type, and approximate country. They do not use cookies or collect personally identifying information.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use advertising cookies or behavioral tracking.

How we use your information

We use the information you share with us to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and plan, book, and support your trips.
  • Send you the Guided Compass newsletter, if you’ve subscribed (you can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of every email).
  • Communicate with travel suppliers (hotels, airlines, transfer providers, tour operators, etc.) on your behalf in the course of booking and supporting your trip.
  • Improve the content and design of our website.

Who we share your information with

  • FORA Travel, our host travel agency, who supports our bookings and operates the intake form.
  • Beehiiv, our newsletter platform, who stores your subscription and sends emails on our behalf.
  • Travel suppliers (hotels, airlines, cruise lines, transfer companies, local guides, etc.) when sharing your information is necessary to book or operate the travel you’ve requested.
  • Service providers such as Vercel (which hosts this website) who process limited technical data needed to deliver the site to your browser.

We do not share your information with anyone else without your permission, except when required by law.

Your rights

You can:

  • Unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time using the link in any newsletter email.
  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request that we delete the personal information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct information that is inaccurate.

To make any of these requests, email info@waylinetravel.com or message Garrett on LinkedIn, and we will respond within a reasonable time.

Children

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has shared information with us, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent change. Significant changes will be communicated through our newsletter or directly to clients we are actively working with.

Contact

Questions, requests, or concerns about your privacy? Email info@waylinetravel.com or reach out on LinkedIn.
